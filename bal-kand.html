Bal ~ Kand



This kand contains the description of the boyhood of Lord Rama, and his adolescence. King Dasarat once went on hunting and his arow struck an innocent boy who was collecting water from the pond. He was the only support to his father and mother who were blind. King Dasarat was guilty and he went away. Later he became the father of 4 sons namely- Ram, Laxman, Bharat, Satrukna. They were all expert in their own field. They went to the gurukul for their studies and grew upto adolescence.
