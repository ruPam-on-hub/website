My book website

This site contains a brief description of my all time favourite story books.

Robinson Crusoe

This story is about a seller. He takes his ship and goes out to explore the world. But god seemed to have other plans for him. He was struck by a massive sea strom and his ship wrecked. He and his crew members barely survived and landed on an island. Later on they found the natives and in exchange of food and fresh water, the became their slave and handed over many precious gold and diamonds.
